why do i need to have an account

creating an account with us give you a lot of advantage. for starters it allows us to send you mails related to astro such as the lauch date, upgrade, release note and future feature to come. secondly it allows the user to use the request function. the request allows users that have verified emails with us ask questions or tell us of feature or customizations they will like in the next version of astro. thirdy it allows user that have account to get notifications through their accounts lastly it allows users that have an account with us to get info about ongoing activities and events that are happening such as the launch, seasonal activities, and many more.

what is email verification

email verification is the process where a users email is verified by us it mean that the users email has been acknowledged by us and will be considered when we are sending out information. email verification does not take a long time to be verified. once your email is verified you can log in to another device with your account and sync your notification and account

how do i get my email verified

getting your email to be verified is as simple as abc its part of the account creation process. once your detail have been inputed in the provided input your default email app will be open and there will be a typed message that will look like this "name: example. email address: example@gmail.com. password: example. status: awaiting verification. verification code: 2c4kc. Info: write the code in your web browser and tap send to complete the verification" the verification code its to be typed in your web browser to complete the first step of the verification. after that send the typed message. this will help us complete the second step of the verification. all the information we collect for email verification is strictly used only for the purpose of verifing your account.

how long does it take to verify my email

email verification doesn't take long. it takes about less than a week to get your email verified, but in some rear cases it might take upto a month to get your email verified.

how do i know if my email has been verified

once your email has been verified you will be sent an email of verification. you can also check if your email has been verfied by checking your account info, there you will see your verification status and whether its awaiting verification or has been verified

what is a request

a request is a message sent by an account holder to us stating what features he or she would like to see in the next version of astro or asking a question. only the request of users whose account has been verified will be taken note of and replied to
